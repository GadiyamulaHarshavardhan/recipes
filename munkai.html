Drumstick Recipe

Video Recipe

Drumstick Curry Ingredients:

lets do it...

  1. Drumsticks: 3 (cut into finger-length pieces
  2. Oil: 3 tablespoons
  3. Onions: 2 (finely chopped, about 1 ½ cups)
  4. Tomatoes: 2 (finely diced, about 1 cup)
  5. Green Chili: 1 (slit, optional; adjust to taste)
  6. Curry Leaves: A sprig
  7. Mustard Seeds: A pinch
  8. Cumin Seeds: ½ teaspoon
  9. Garlic: 2 cloves (crushed, optional; adds a different flavor)
  10. Red Chili Powder: 1 teaspoon (adjust to your preferred spice level)
  11. Turmeric Powder: ¼ teaspoon
  12. Salt: To taste
  13. Coriander Powder: 1 teaspoon (optional)
  14. Water: 1 cup (adjust as needed)

Instructions To Do:

lets do it:
1.Sauté the Aromatics

  • Heat oil in a deep pan.
  • Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add curry leaves, crushed garlic, and green chili. Sauté until the leaves turn crisp.
  • Add finely chopped onions and sprinkle some salt. Fry until the onions turn transparent (no need to brown them).

2.Add Drumsticks and Tomatoes:

  • Add the drumstick pieces and fry for 2 to 3 minutes to remove the raw smell.
  • Add the diced tomatoes, more salt, and turmeric. Fry until the tomatoes become completely soft and pulpy (no need to cover; frying in an open pan tastes better).

3.Spice it Up:

  • Add red chili powder and coriander powder (optional). Saute well until the raw smell of the chili powder disappears.

4.Cook with Water:

  • Pour water just enough to cover the drumsticks. Stir well.
  • Cook on medium flame until the drumsticks are thoroughly cooked. For even better flavor, try cooking on the lowest heat for a longer time.

5.Serve and Enjoy:

  • Serve the drumstick curry with rice or chapati. You can also add boiled eggs (pricked with a fork) when the drumsticks are almost cooked for an extra twist.
