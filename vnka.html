Brinjal Recipe

video link

ingredients

lets do it....

  • Brinjal (eggplant) - 250 grams
  • (cubed)Onions - 1 large or 2 medium
  • (thinly sliced)Tomatoes - 1 large or 2 medium
  • Green chili - 1
  • oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Mustard seeds - ¼ teaspoon
  • Cumin seeds - ½ teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder - ¼ teaspoon
  • salt - to taste
  • curry leaves - a few
  • Coriander leaves (cilantro) - for garnish

recipe

steps to follow:
1.Prepare the Brinjals:

  • Wash and cube the brinjals (eggplants). Immerse them in water to prevent bitterness and discoloration. You can add a pinch of salt to the water to further prevent bitterness.
  • Chop the onions, tomatoes, and green chili.

2.Make the Onion-Tomato Masala

  • Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pot.
  • Add ¼ teaspoon of mustard seeds and ½ teaspoon of cumin seeds.
  • Once they splutter, add the sliced onions. Sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  • Add the diced tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.

3.Spice it Up:

  • Add the following spice powders:
  • ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder (adjust to your preferred spice level)
  • Cumin powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Mix well.

4.Add the Brinjals

  • Add the cubed brinjals and green chili
  • Mix everything together.

5.Cook and Garnish:

  • Pour in 1 ½ cups of water.
  • Cook the mixture until you achieve a thick gravy consistency.
  • Garnish with fresh curry leaves and coriander leaves.

6.Serve:

  • Serve your Brinjal Curry hot with rice, roti, or any Indian flatbread of your choice.
